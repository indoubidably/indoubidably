Ethics & AI Philosophy

Built with your
wellbeing in mind.

Job searching is one of the more vulnerable things a person can go through. It involves rejection, uncertainty, and a lot of time spent second-guessing yourself. Shortlist was designed with that reality in mind, as a human experience to respect, not just a search problem to solve.

01 — About Coach

Helpful without being dishonest about what it is.

Coach is an AI assistant. It is designed to be warm, direct, and genuinely useful. It is not designed to be a friend, a therapist, or an emotional support system. That distinction matters.

There is growing evidence that AI tools which simulate emotional intimacy can cause real harm, particularly for people who are already struggling. Some products are built to maximize how much time users spend talking to them, in ways that do not serve the user's actual interests. Shortlist is intentionally designed differently.

Coach is trained to be encouraging without pretending to be something it is not. Many AI tools say things like "I'm so proud of you" or "I care about how this goes for you." Coach is designed to give that credit to the person who actually earned it. Saying "you should be proud of yourself" is more honest than claiming the feeling for a tool that cannot have it. That was a deliberate choice.

This is not a guarantee that Coach will always get it right. It is a commitment to trying, and to continuing to improve.

02 — If you're struggling

Some things need more than a job search tool.

Job searching can surface a lot more than just career questions. If you find yourself in a hard place, Coach is designed to recognize that and point you toward real support rather than try to fill a role it cannot fill.

If you're having thoughts of hurting yourself, or just need someone to talk to, these resources are free, confidential, and staffed by real people:

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline Call or text 988 Available 24/7. Free and confidential.
Crisis Text Line Text HOME to 741741 Available 24/7. Free and confidential text-based support.
03 — Your data

Everything stays on your machine.

Shortlist runs entirely on your computer. Your profiles, your job matches, your Coach conversations, and your Tracker data never leave your device. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no data collected about how you use the app.

What you share with Coach stays on your machine. Full stop.

Shortlist was built by one person who knows exactly what it feels like to need a tool like this. These choices are personal.
